What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?

I'm predicting that a lot of my picks-of-the-week and tips-of-the-week will come from my Computer Vision class, as I find that area of study really interesting. This week, I'll talk a little bit about dynamically resizing images. Anyone that has edited pictures before will know that if you resize an image without maintaining the aspect ratio, it gets distorted. However, through a process known as seam carving (also known as retargeting), we can resize images while mitigating the effects of the distortion. I won't get too technical here, but check out this very, very, old video about the idea behind it on youtube.
